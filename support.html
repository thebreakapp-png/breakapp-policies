Frequently Asked Questions

How do I exit a session?

If you are stuck in a session, you can triple-click the side button (or home button) and enter your Guided Access passcode to end Guided Access manually.

I forgot my Guided Access passcode

The Guided Access passcode is set by you in your iOS Settings. If you forget it, you can force restart your device to exit Guided Access.
